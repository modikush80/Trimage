## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does Trimage delete my original photos?**
No. Trimage never deletes anything automatically. After converting, it moves your
original files into an album ("RAW Originals" or "Compressed Originals") so you
can review them and delete them yourself in the Photos app whenever you're ready.

**Are my photos uploaded anywhere?**
No. All processing happens entirely on your device. Trimage does not collect,
upload, or share your photos or any personal data.

**How do I give Trimage access to my photos?**
Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → Trimage, and choose Full Access.

**Why are some photos skipped?**
In RAW mode, only RAW/DNG photos are converted. In Compress mode, only JPEGs
above your chosen size threshold are processed (HEIC photos are left alone
because converting them would increase file size).

**Where did my freed-up space go?**
Storage is reclaimed once you delete the originals from the album in the Photos
app, after you've confirmed you're happy with the converted versions.
