# Trimage — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 15, 2026_

Trimage ("the app") is designed to protect your privacy. This policy explains
what the app does and does not do with your data.

## Summary

Trimage processes all photos **entirely on your device**. The app does not
collect, store, transmit, or share your photos or any personal information with
the developer or any third party. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no
tracking.

## Photo Library Access

Trimage requests access to your photo library for one purpose: to let you
convert and compress the photos you choose. Specifically, the app uses this
access to:

- Find RAW/DNG photos and large JPEGs in your library.
- Create converted or recompressed JPEG copies that you initiate.
- Optionally organize original files into an album so you can review and delete
  them yourself in the Photos app.

All of this happens locally on your device. Trimage never uploads your photos
anywhere. If a selected photo is stored in iCloud, the system may download it to
your device so it can be processed — this is handled by Apple's Photos
framework and the photo is not sent to the developer.

You can change or revoke photo access at any time in
**Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos**.

## Data Collection

Trimage does **not** collect any data. The app has no servers, does not use
analytics or advertising SDKs, and does not track you across apps or websites.

The app stores a small amount of non-personal information **on your device only**
(for example, your cumulative "space saved" statistics and your quality
settings). This information never leaves your device and is removed if you
delete the app.

## Children's Privacy

Trimage does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.
